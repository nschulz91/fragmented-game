# Fragmented Story

## Premise

Charlie Smith is tracking the force that kidnapped his brother. The first reliable lead points to Lake Pixor, a corrupted battleground in Parxillia where the Shadow Court is already testing how far Charlie can be pushed before he breaks.

## Charlie Smith

- White skin
- Jet-black hair
- Pink eyes
- Appearance shifts slightly when his power spikes
- Can alter time and parts of the universe

## Core Threat

The Prince of Shadows took Charlie's brother and is building layers of protection between Charlie and the Shadow Castle. Lake Pixor is the first direct countermeasure: toxic water, staged enemy waves, and a warden strong enough to stop anyone who survives the approach.

## Version 1 Story Beat

Charlie enters Lake Pixor, survives two enemy pushes, defeats the resident warden, and confirms the Prince of Shadows is actively defending the trail. Winning this encounter does not end the story. It proves Charlie is strong enough to keep going.
